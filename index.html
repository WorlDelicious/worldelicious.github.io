How it works

Register yourself and start interacting with a community that shares the same food tastes as you. You're missing a local product for your receipe ? No worries, a delivery will be arranged. (YES, even from the other side of the world!)
+ Imagine a world where you could connect to your roots wherever you are.
+ Imagine a world where you could have whatever ingredients wherever you need them.
+ Imagine a world where you could connect to other cultures and other gastonomies.
At WorlDelicious, we believe that we need to connect with our roots and be able to receive packages from home as mum would do them. At WorlDeliciousm, we want to enable you to remember your travels through the food you discovered. And last but not least, we believe people can make people happy by delivering the ingredients they need.
